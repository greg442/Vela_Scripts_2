## What the Installer Does

The installer sets up your complete VELA system in 18 automated steps:

1. Installs all dependencies (Homebrew, Ollama, OpenClaw)
2. Downloads local AI models (~15–30 minutes)
3. Validates your VELA license
4. Configures Telegram, WhatsApp, and Google Workspace
5. Creates your AI team (Hannah + 5 specialist agents)
6. Sets up automated email triage, daily briefs, and backups
7. Starts and verifies the system

When it finishes, Hannah is live in Telegram.
